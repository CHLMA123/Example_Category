With the free WinBook NVR APP, you can turn your Smartphone into a monitoring center for your NVR. When you first enter the app, you will see the screen shown below. You are now ready to add your device.
You can add and manage your NVR on this page.
Tapicon to pop up the following interface.
You can choose one of the threes ways to add your device.
Way 1：Search On LAN
Step1 If your smartphone and NVR are within the same LAN, you can choose the first way "Search On LAN" to search your device on LAN.
Step2 Enter "Search Device" interface, this interface will display the devices which have been searched, click the searched device.
Step 3 Enter "Add New Device" inerface, you need to input the username and password of your NVR, then click "Connect" to connect your device.
Way 2：Search QR Code
You also can choose "Scan QR Code" to add your device.
Scan the QR code on the bottom of the NVR. You can also tap "Album" to select the picture of QR Code in your album.
When you finish the QR code scanning, the APP will pop-up device information page automatically. Input the username and password of your NVR, then click "Connect" to connect your device.
Way 3：Manual DDNS/IP Address
You also can choose "Manual DDNS/IP" to add your device. Via this way, you need to input UID, DDNS or IP Address manual.
Input one of the UID, DDNS or IP Address information to "P2P UID / DDNS / IP Address" input box, then input the username and password of your NVR, then click "Connect" to connect your device.
When the app has successfully connected with your NVR.
Tap the icon to play Video.
If the app fails to connect, make sure the UID and username / password of your NVR are corrected. Your will now see a live view of all your cameras connected.
